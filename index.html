BH Merger Waveform Explorer

Model: a fast, browser-friendly pedagogical IMR-like waveform (inspiral chirp + smooth merger + damped ringdown). “Detector view” applies a soft bandpass in frequency space (cosine ramps) plus a time taper to reduce edge/ringing artifacts. Audio uses a simple resampling + normalization for an audible “chirp” impression.

Tip: heavier masses → shorter / “lower pitched”. Raise f_low → you lose early inspiral. Lower f_high → merger/ringdown is smoothed away. For audio: increase “speed” to make it more audible.
